OUR PROJECT ON TEA LEAVES DISEASE

Tea leaves pictures can be used for the Prediction of various tea leaves disease which causes huge loss in agricultural system every year. The proposed system - Tea leaves image processing System helps us to the analysis of leaves and there by help us in Predicting various leaves diseases. The input to the proposed system is the Tea leaves image. The leaves portion is segmented and leaves color. shape and texture features are extracted and taken together to form a feature vector and then analysis of leaves are done which is used for the diagnosis of various tea leaves diseases. This proposed system will help the farmers in the early diagnosis of diseases...
